Background

We are a pair of Computer Science students from the National University of Singapore (NUS) who decided to partake in Orbital, which is NUSâ€™ annual summer software development module for freshmen. After countless discussions, we chose to work on the Slim Jeans Recipe Calorie Counter due to the wide range of possibilities it presented to us. It allowed us to work on something useful while also being versatile enough to expand upon if we had the time to do so. At its core, the website targets those who would like to track their caloric intake based on recipes that they are interested in preparing.
